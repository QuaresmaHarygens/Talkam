# Talkam Liberia Promotional Copy

## App Store Descriptions
**Short (80 chars):**
Report issues safely, even offline. Verified alerts for every Liberian county.

**Long:**
Talkam Liberia is the secure way to report roadblocks, violence, disasters, or corruption anywhere in Liberia. Capture photos, videos, or masked voice notes, even when offline. Your report syncs when you reconnect and stays encrypted end-to-end. Get crisis alerts for your county, follow response timelines, and chat anonymously with trusted NGOs, journalists, or government teams. Built with Liberian English localization, data minimization, and moderation that protects witnesses from hate speech or doxxing.

## Press Release
**Headline:** Talkam Liberia Launches Privacy-First Social Reporting Network for Every County

**Paragraph 1:** Talkam Liberia today introduced a nationwide platform that lets citizens, community reporters, and responders surface incidents securely—even on low-bandwidth devices. The app combines anonymous tokens, offline queues, and SMS ingestion so Liberians can "talkam" from anywhere.

**Paragraph 2:** Built with civil society partners, Talkam Liberia filters hate speech, masks sensitive media, and routes urgent cases to verified NGOs, journalists, and government agencies. Dashboards deliver live heatmaps, verification workflows, and exportable evidence trails to accelerate aid and accountability.

**Paragraph 3:** The team invites NGOs, newsrooms, and agencies to join the pilot advisory council, access premium analytics, and co-design escalation playbooks tailored to Liberia’s realities.
