# Talkam Liberia User Guide

## Welcome to Talkam Liberia

Talkam Liberia is your platform to report issues, share concerns, and help make Liberia better. This guide will help you get started.

## Getting Started

### Downloading the App

1. **Android**: Search "Talkam Liberia" in Google Play Store
2. **iOS**: Search "Talkam Liberia" in App Store
3. **SMS Alternative**: Send reports via SMS to **8888** (free)

### First-Time Setup

1. **Open the app** after installation
2. **Choose how to use**:
   - **Sign Up** (with phone number) - for full features
   - **Use Anonymously** - for maximum privacy

3. **Grant permissions**:
   - Location (to tag your reports)
   - Camera (to add photos)
   - Storage (to save reports offline)

## Creating Your First Report

### Step 1: Tap "Create Report"

The plus (+) button on the home screen.

### Step 2: Choose Category

- **Infrastructure**: Roads, bridges, water, electricity
- **Security**: Safety concerns, incidents
- **Health**: Healthcare access, emergencies
- **Education**: Schools, access issues
- **Environment**: Pollution, waste, flooding

### Step 3: Describe the Issue

- Write a clear summary (required)
- Add details if needed (optional)
- Set severity level (Low/Medium/High)

### Step 4: Add Location

- **Automatic**: App uses your current location
- **Manual**: Tap map to select location
- **County**: Select from dropdown

### Step 5: Add Media (Optional)

- **Photo**: Take picture or choose from gallery
- **Video**: Record or upload (max 30 seconds)
- **Audio**: Voice recording (max 60 seconds)

**Privacy**: Faces and voices are automatically blurred/masked.

### Step 6: Submit

- Tap "Submit Report"
- If offline, report will sync automatically when online

## Viewing Reports

### Home Feed

- See all recent reports from your county
- Filter by category, severity, or status
- Pull down to refresh

### Map View

- See reports on an interactive map
- Filter by category
- Tap markers for details
- Enable heatmap for patterns

### My Reports

- View your submitted reports
- Check verification status
- See comments from officials

## Staying Safe

### Anonymous Mode

- No personal information stored
- Reports linked to device only
- Cannot be traced back to you

### Verified Reports

- Reports verified by trusted organizations
- Green checkmark indicates verification
- Higher priority for response

### Emergency Contacts

Access emergency hotlines from Settings:
- Police: **911**
- Fire: **911**
- Medical: **911**
- National Security: **+231 XXX-XXXX**

## Offline Mode

### Automatic Sync

- Reports saved locally when offline
- Automatically sync when online
- No data loss

### Manual Sync

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Tap **Sync Reports**
3. Wait for confirmation

## Tips for Effective Reporting

1. **Be Specific**: Clear descriptions help responders
2. **Add Photos**: Visual evidence increases credibility
3. **Correct Location**: Accurate location ensures proper routing
4. **Appropriate Severity**: Use High for emergencies only
5. **Follow Up**: Check your report status regularly

## Privacy & Security

- Your data is encrypted
- Anonymous option available
- No location tracking when app closed
- Media automatically anonymized
